# Portledge School Student Handbook Summary

## Mission Statement
Portledge School empowers students to become confident, creative thinkers and compassionate global citizens through a rigorous, student-centered education.

## Core Values
- **Excellence**: Pursue the highest standards in academics and character
- **Respect**: Value diverse perspectives and treat all community members with dignity
- **Integrity**: Act honestly and ethically in all endeavors
- **Community**: Foster collaboration and support among students, faculty, and families
- **Innovation**: Embrace creativity and adaptability in learning

## Academic Policies

### Grading Scale
- A: 93-100 (Excellent)
- A-: 90-92
- B+: 87-89
- B: 83-86
- B-: 80-82
- C+: 77-79
- C: 73-76
- C-: 70-72
- D: 65-69
- F: Below 65

### Honor Code
All students must adhere to academic honesty principles:
- Submit only your own work
- Cite all sources appropriately
- Do not plagiarize or cheat
- Report honor code violations

### Homework Policy
- Complete all assignments on time
- Late work may receive reduced credit
- Communicate with teachers if you need an extension
- Average homework time: 2-3 hours per night

## Student Life

### Dress Code
- Business casual attire Monday-Thursday
- Casual Friday (within guidelines)
- School logo apparel encouraged
- Athletic wear only in PE/athletics

### Technology Use
- Laptops required for classes
- Cell phones allowed during breaks/lunch
- No phones during class without permission
- Respect digital citizenship guidelines

### Attendance
- School starts at 8:00 AM
- Must arrive on time to all classes
- Absences require parent note
- More than 3 unexcused absences may affect credit
